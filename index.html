Personal work

VR gym project (WIP)

This is the VR project I have been working on during my sabbatical. I started out with the intention to expand my knowledge of the unreal engine as well as try out VR development. I feel I have come a long way and even with the struggles I feel it was worth it. And will continue to work on this because even now I can think of many ways to improve what has been done. The project was made in UE 4.27.2 and made specifically for the meta quest 2. Recorded footage comes out brighter than in-game and emissive lighting takes a hit. Nevertheless, these vids will give a good idea about the in-game mechanics.

Professional Projects

Take On Mars

During my time on Take On Mars my main focus was environments. I designed, prototyped, modelled and textured the 1st modular base seen in game. I also re-designed and made 3 later variations. In addition I did some asset work, including designing , modelling and animating the 3D printer and some vehicle work (concepting, modelling and texturing).

DayZ

My work on DayZ included: modelling and texturing props and buildings, making resolution LODs for building done by other artists, bug fixing, setting up collisions and other functionality in the engine.
